South Carolina Fires

A simple, accessible dashboard for fire activity across the state.

Welcome

South Carolina Fires was built to make it easier to track and understand fire activity in South Carolina. The site pulls data from NASA FIRMS and presents recent fire detections in a clear, accessible way.

About Me

Hi, I'm Jill Platts, a developer and educator who loves combining technology and design to make small, meaningful projects. This site is a personal project created to provide timely and accessible fire information to the public.
